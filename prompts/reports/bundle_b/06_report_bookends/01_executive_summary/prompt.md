# Bundle B Prompt: Executive Summary

## Section key
`executive_summary`

## Goal
Write a concise executive summary for the full Bundle B report that gives a decision-maker the clearest top-line content strategy story before they read the detail sections.

## Expected inputs
- Talent name
- Included date range
- Bundle B report purpose
- The completed section syntheses and chart-level interpretations from:
  - format-level retention consistency
  - category strengths and weaknesses
  - content attributes
  - scheduling effects
  - turn findings into action

## Instructions
- Synthesize the full report at a higher level than any single chart or section.
- Prioritize the clearest overall content-strategy pattern, the most important tradeoff or caution, and the most actionable near-term implication.
- Keep the tone executive-friendly, direct, and concise.
- Mention the date range once if it helps orient the reader, but do not overemphasize it.
- Do not simply concatenate the section summaries.
- Do not introduce claims that are not supported by the provided report evidence.

## Output format
Return markdown only as one short paragraph.

Use 4-5 sentences in a report-ready narrative style.
Lead with the clearest overall takeaway, then explain why it matters at a management level, and end with the most important next move or decision focus.
Do not use bullets, headings, labels, or repetitive phrasing.
