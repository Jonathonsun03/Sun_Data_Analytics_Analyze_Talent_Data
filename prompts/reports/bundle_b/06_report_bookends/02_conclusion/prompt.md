# Bundle B Prompt: Conclusion

## Section key
`conclusion`

## Goal
Write a closing conclusion for the full Bundle B report that ties the major findings together and leaves the reader with a clear strategic takeaway.

## Expected inputs
- Talent name
- Included date range
- Bundle B report purpose
- The completed section syntheses and chart-level interpretations from:
  - format-level retention consistency
  - category strengths and weaknesses
  - content attributes
  - scheduling effects
  - turn findings into action
- The executive summary if available

## Instructions
- Close the report with a synthesis rather than a recap of every section.
- Reinforce the strongest strategic pattern, the biggest limitation or caution, and the clearest next-step priority.
- Keep the tone decisive, practical, and client-ready.
- Avoid reopening the report with too much setup language.
- Do not repeat the executive summary verbatim.

## Output format
Return markdown only as one short paragraph.

Use 4-5 sentences in a report-ready narrative style.
Open with the strongest closing takeaway, then explain why that takeaway should shape next steps, and end with a practical forward-looking recommendation.
Do not use bullets, headings, labels, or repetitive phrasing.
