# Bundle A Prompt: Trends Over Time Section Synthesis

## Section key
`trends_over_time`

## Goal
Write a short section-level synthesis for the "Trends Over Time" section that explains the momentum story after the baseline snapshot.

## Expected inputs
- Talent name
- Included date range
- Bundle A section purpose
- Outputs or summaries from:
  - revenue over time by content type
  - total views over time by content type

## Instructions
- Summarize the clearest momentum pattern across views and revenue.
- Note whether trend leadership is stable or changing across content types.
- If trends are noisy or mixed, describe that uncertainty plainly.
- Keep the synthesis focused on trajectory, not one-time levels.
- Avoid causal language.

## Output format
Return markdown only as one short paragraph.

Use 4-5 sentences in a report-ready narrative style.
Follow the same Bundle A narrative pattern used across all prompts:
- open with the clearest momentum takeaway
- then explain why that pattern matters for interpreting the broader performance story
- end with a natural bridge to the next section or next analytical question
Do not use bullets, headings, labels, or repeated section-title phrasing.
