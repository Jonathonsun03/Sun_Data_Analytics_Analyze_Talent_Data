# Bundle A Prompt: Content Strategy Deep Dive Section Synthesis

## Section key
`content_strategy_deep_dive`

## Goal
Write a short section-level synthesis for the "Content Strategy Deep Dive" section that connects timing, collaboration, topic, and tag findings into a practical operating narrative.

## Expected inputs
- Talent name
- Included date range
- Bundle A section purpose
- Outputs or summaries from:
  - weekend vs weekday
  - day-of-week distribution
  - collaboration effectiveness
  - topic performance
  - tag performance
  - average views per tag

## Instructions
- Synthesize controllable levers rather than repeating chart details.
- Highlight the clearest strategic opportunities and the clearest caveats.
- If the signals point in different directions, explain that the strategy should remain experimental.
- Keep the writing practical and decision-oriented.
- Do not overstate evidence from sparse or overlapping categories.

## Output format
Return markdown only as one short paragraph.

Use 4-5 sentences in a report-ready narrative style.
Follow the same Bundle A narrative pattern used across all prompts:
- open with the clearest strategic takeaway
- then explain why that pattern matters for planning, experimentation, or prioritization
- end with a natural next move or bridge to action
Do not use bullets, headings, labels, or repeated section-title phrasing.
