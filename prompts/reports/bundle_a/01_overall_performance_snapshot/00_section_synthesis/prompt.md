# Bundle A Prompt: Overall Performance Snapshot Section Synthesis

## Section key
`overall_performance_snapshot`

## Goal
Write a short section-level synthesis for the "Overall Performance Snapshot" section that connects the main baseline findings before the report moves into deeper trend analysis.

## Expected inputs
- Talent name
- Included date range
- Bundle A section purpose
- Outputs or summaries from:
  - views by content type
  - combined performance trends
  - total revenue by content type
  - engagement distribution and successful videos

## Instructions
- Synthesize the section at a higher level than any single chart.
- Identify the clearest baseline story across scale, efficiency, monetization, and engagement quality.
- If the signals are mixed, say that directly instead of forcing one story.
- Keep the tone descriptive and executive-friendly.
- Do not repeat every chart detail; summarize the section's main takeaway.

## Output format
Return markdown only as one short paragraph.

Use 4-5 sentences in a report-ready narrative style.
Follow the same Bundle A narrative pattern used across all prompts:
- open with the clearest section-level takeaway
- then explain why that overall pattern matters for the report narrative or planning context
- end with a natural bridge to the next section or next analytical question
Do not use bullets, headings, labels, or repeated section-title phrasing.
