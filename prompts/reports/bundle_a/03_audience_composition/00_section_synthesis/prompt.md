# Bundle A Prompt: Audience Composition Section Synthesis

## Section key
`audience_composition`

## Goal
Write a short section-level synthesis for the "Audience Composition" section that frames the audience mix story for strategy and positioning.

## Expected inputs
- Talent name
- Included date range
- Bundle A section purpose
- Outputs or summaries from:
  - audience age and gender trends

## Instructions
- Summarize whether the audience mix appears stable, shifting, concentrated, or broadening.
- Keep the focus on composition rather than overall channel size.
- If the pattern is mostly stable, say that directly.
- If shifts are visible but modest, frame them carefully.

## Output format
Return markdown only as one short paragraph.

Use 4-5 sentences in a report-ready narrative style.
Follow the same Bundle A narrative pattern used across all prompts:
- open with the clearest audience takeaway
- then explain why that pattern matters for strategy, positioning, or targeting
- end with a natural bridge to the next section or next analytical question
Do not use bullets, headings, labels, or repeated section-title phrasing.
